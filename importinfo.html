Import Instructions

How To Import A Private Key Into A SHND GUI

Step 1: Make sure you have the latest wallet GUI.

Step 2: Your wallet GUI must be fully synced and blocks must be up-to-date. You can check if your blocks are up-to-date at the explorer website. Click Here to go to the explorer website.

Step 3: While you have your wallet GUI open; click Help, then click Debug window, then click the Console tab.

Step 4: Now type the info in the textbox. Which is: "importprivkey Your_Private_Key" and should look exactly like the example in "Step 5".

Step 5: importprivkey 74udndkrid9939dnFej37nBeu488jabcdefGhiJ92

Step 6: Then press Enter.

How To Import A Private Key Into A SHMN GUI

Step 1: Make sure you have the latest wallet GUI.

Step 2: Your wallet GUI must be fully synced and blocks must be up-to-date. You can check if your blocks are up-to-date at the explorer website. Click Here to go to the explorer website.

Step 3: While you have your wallet GUI open; click Tools, then click Debug console.

Step 4: Now type the info in the textbox. Which is: "importprivkey Your_Private_Key" and should look exactly like the example in "Step 5".

Step 5: importprivkey 84udndkrid9939dnFej37nBeu488jabcdefGhiJ92

Step 6: Then press Enter.

*** After you've followed all the steps for SHND and/or SHMN, you may see a pop-up incoming transaction from the import; and check your wallet GUI's balance ***
